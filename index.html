Welcome to Mathematics of Data Science (MathODS), a virtual conference organized by graduate students taking place on the 11-12th June, 2020.

MathODS aims to provide a venue for graduate students and early career researchers to share their exciting, novel, and thought-provoking research ideas and most importantly, to facilitate interactive discussusions.

We will allow registered participants to live stream on Zoom. We invite contributions in the form of a talk or a poster presentation, see Program for more information.

The mathematical foundations of this event will lie between probability, statistics, optimization, and image processing broadly organized into four categories:

  1. Optimisation

  2. Mathematics of deep learning

  3. Bayesian methods

  4. Application of machine learning in life sciences
