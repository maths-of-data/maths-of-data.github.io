Types of contributions

MathODS will provide a venue for two types of contribution.

Contributed talk (20 min. + 10 min. questions, single track)

Talks will be 20 min. long followed by up to 10 min. of questions and presented in a single track session for each of the four topic areas.

Poster presentation (50 min., multi track)

Posters will be presented in a 50 min. session for each of the four topic areas. During the sessions participants will be able to join various Zoom rooms of the individual presenters. Poster sessions are aimed to allow for a more interactive presentations and can go beyond showing a PDF of a poster, e.g. can include code demonstrations, etc.
