Types of contributions

The main goal of the conference is to facilitate discussions among graduate students. As such, we invite participants to contribute in one of the two ways.

Contributed talk (20 min. + 10 min. questions, single track)

There will be five talks for each of the four topic areas in a single track session.
Talks will be 20 minutes long followed by up to 10 minutes of questions.

Poster presentation (50 min., multi track)

Each of the four topic areas will have a poster presentation which will take 50 minutes, during which participants will be able to join various Zoom sessions of the presenters. These are aimed to allow for interactive presentations and can go beyond showing a PDF of a poster, e.g. can include code demonstrations, etc.
